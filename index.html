About Me

This website is a space for me to share projects, ideas, and things I'm interested in professionally and personally.

I’m Connor, and I’ve always taken pride in being someone others can count on. Whether leading a team, managing a project, or working independently, I bring a thoughtful and capable approach to getting things done. My background includes hands on leadership roles and labor intensive work in fast-paced, long-shift environments with tightly coordinated teams. Reliability, communication, and follow-through are core to how I operate shaping both my day-to-day work and my overall mindset. I find joy in learning new things and even more from teaching people something new. Outside of work, I’m an avid skier, rock climber, golfer, and traveler. Some of my most memorable experiences include hiking and climbing all around Greece in 2024, moving to Banff to ski and work full time, and golfing some of the best North American from Phoenix and Naples to Banff. I enjoy living an active lifestyle and challenging myself in new ways, including training for the Toronto Hyrox race in October 2025, as well as climbing my home crag's of Metcalf and Old Baldy.

I’m building this site with the help of AI tools, a tight budget, and limited coding experience. By learning as I go and figuring things out one step at a time, I’ve managed to bootstrap this project with minimal time and money. It’s still a work in progress, and that’s part of the point. This process has shown me how powerful AI can be as a practical support tool, not just for individuals like me building from scratch, but for businesses of any size. I believe AI isn’t just a shortcut or a convenience but a transformative force capable of redefining how we solve problems, approach creativity, and expand what we believe is possible. What once felt out of reach is now attainable for anyone willing to take the leap.
