The Website Project

My self-built portfolio site developed with HTML, Python, CSS, and AI tools to showcase my projects and personal work.

This website began as a blank canvas as a personal challenge to see how far I could go without relying on drag-and-drop builders or expensive subscriptions. I started with zero web development experience and used AI tools, curiosity, and persistence to bring it to life. Every section, feature, and bug fix taught me something new about design, code, and creative problem-solving. More than just a portfolio, this project reflects my values: practical learning, doing things the hard way when it’s worth it, and proving that initiative and resourcefulness go a long way.
